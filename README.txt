## Installation Guide

### Pre Requirment Plugin
1. **Elementor**
2. **Turbo Addons Elementor** - Its free and avilable on wordpress.org and free plugin list in dashboard

Follow these steps to install Turbo Addons Pro for Elementor on your WordPress website:

1. **Download the Plugin:**
   - Purchase `turbo-addons-elementor-pro.zip` file from [Turbo Addons website](https://turbo-addons.com/) or AppSumo.

2. **Upload the Plugin:**
   - Log into your WordPress admin dashboard.
   - Navigate to Plugins > Add New.
   - Click Upload Plugin and select the `turbo-addons-elementor-pro.zip` file. [!Important]: File name must be same as turbo-addons-elementor-pro.zip

3. **Activate the Plugin:**
   - Once the installation is complete, click `Activate` to enable the plugin.

## How to Use Turbo Addons Pro for Elementor

1. **Add a Widget:**
   - Open any post or page in the Elementor editor.
   - In the Elementor widget panel, locate the **Turbo Addons Pro** category.
   - Drag and drop the desired widget into your layout.

2. **Customize Widget Settings:**
   - Click on the widget to access its customization options.
   - Adjust design, layout, and settings to fit your needs.

3. **Publish or Update:**
   - After completing customization, click `Publish` or `Update` to save changes.
